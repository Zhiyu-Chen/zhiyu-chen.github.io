<p>This paper introduce Relational Graph Convolutional Networks (<strong>R-GCNs</strong>) which deal with relational data. In addition, techniques for parameter sharing and to enforce sparsity constraints are introduced to apply R-GCNs to multigraphs with large numbers of relations.</p>

<p>To solve this problem, a graph auto-encoder model is introduced.  The <strong>encoder </strong>maps an entity to a real-valued vector and the <strong>decoder </strong>reconstruct edges by a scoring function.
